Johannes Philippus Booyens


Stock farmer, Smithfield (1883)
Born Circa 1862

Notes:

At the time of his marriage, Johannes Philippus is a stock farmer in the Smithfield district. Indeed, the Membership Books of the Smithfield Dutch Reformed Church [#1] show him as being confirmed in that church on 30 August 1884. The book also shows him as departing for Dewetsdorp on 10 Feb 1885.
