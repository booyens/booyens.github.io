Cornelia Christina Visagie


Born February 1846, Philippolis, OFS 1
Died 21 May 1933, Epako, District of Omaruru, South West Africa 2

Notes:

Cornelia is the widow of Johan Peter Carl von Maltitz. The couple had an own natural son, who died in 1894, and an adopted son named Ludiwg Johan Frederich. When Willem Jacobus Booyens dies in 1913, Cornelia moves to South West Africa (Namibia) where she lives out the final years of her life on her son Ludwig's farm, Epako. She passes away in 1933 on that farm.

Married Circa 11 May 1897 at Johannesburg 3
