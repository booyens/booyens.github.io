Tjaart Johannes Booyens


Born 3 December 1852, Bloemfontein 1
Died 17 June 1908, Klipplaatdrif, Ventersdrop, District Potchefstroom

Notes:

Tjaart is named for his great-great grandfather, Tjaart van der Walt (1748-1802), the historically famous Field-Cornet of the Swellendam Burghers who died in battle with the Khoekhoe in the rugged country behind Patensie in the South Cape. The naming is perfectly in keeping with the Afrikaner Naming Convention.

Married 6 January 1872 at Potchefstroom
